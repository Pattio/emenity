The Last Fortress

Last Fortress is simple, yet really entertaining and interesting game, which focuses on improving player's reaction. In the middle of the battle player has to quickly change and upgrade forts, cast magic skill, build barriers in order to defeat hord of enemies, coming from everywhere. Player progress is saved during the game meaning that every decision can affect future gameplay. Variety of enemies is astonishing ranging from simple zombies to angry grandmas carrying bombs and explosives.
